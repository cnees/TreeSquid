How it Works
Features

Making a conversation

  1. Click "New Conversation" in the top left
  2. Enter a topic
  3. Enter a message
  4. Click "Create"

Replying

  1. Click on any message to reply to it
  2. Enter a response
  3. Click submit

Navigating

  • Click and drag the background to pan
  • Zoom in and out with the '+' and '-' buttons in the upper right
  • Zoom and out with a trackpad or scroll wheel
  • Reset zoom with the diagonal arrow icon in the upper right

Picking a Conversation

  • Conversations you've participated in are on the left beneath the "New Conversation" button
  • The current conversation is highlighted
  • The first line shows the conversation topic
  • The second line shows a preview of the last message you posted in the conversation
